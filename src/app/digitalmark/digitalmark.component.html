Here's a quick rundown of some of the most common digital marketing tactics.

Search Engine Optimization (SEO)


This is the process of optimizing your website to "rank" higher in search engine results pages, thereby increasing the amount of organic (or free) traffic your website receives. There are a number of ways to approach SEO in order to generate qualified traffic to your website.

• On page SEO
• Off page SEO
• Technical SEO

Content Marketing


This term denotes the creation and promotion of content assets. The channels that can play a part in your content marketing strategy include:

• Blog posts
• Ebooks and whitepapers
• Infographics

Social Media Marketing


This practice promotes your brand and your content on social media channels to increase brand awareness, drive traffic, and generate leads for your business. The channels include:

  •  Facebook
  •  Twitter
  •  LinkedIn
  •  Instagram
  •  Snapchat
  •  Pinterest

Pay Per Click (PPC)


PPC is a method of driving traffic to your website by paying a publisher every time your ad is clicked. Examples
• Google Ads
• Paid ads on Facebook
• Twitter Ads campaigns
• Sponsored Messages on LinkedIn

Affiliate Marketing


In this you receive commission for promoting someone else's products or services on your website. It includes:

• Hosting video ads through the YouTube Partner Program.
• Posting affiliate links from your social media accounts.

Native Advertising


Native advertising refers to advertisements that are primarily content-led and featured on a platform alongside other, non-paid content. Example: BuzzFeed-sponsored posts

Marketing Automation


It refers to the software that serves to automate your basic marketing operations. Such as:

• Email newsletters
• Social media post scheduling
• Lead-nurturing workflows
• Campaign tracking and reporting

Email Marketing


It is often used to promote content, discounts and events.

Online PR


It is the practice of securing earned online coverage with blogs and other content-based websites.

Inbound Marketing


Inbound marketing refers to a marketing methodology wherein you engage and delight customers at every stage of the buyer's journey.
